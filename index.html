About Us

Global parts International Inc. (GPII) is a parts warehouse specializing in selling parts for antique cars. The company has developed a reputation of being able to fin or carry almost any part for older automobiles produced in the USA since 1945 through 1990. All parts they carry are for cars that are at least 25 years old.

GPII still manages its inventory the old fashioned way. Customers call their 800 number and place orders. GPII do have a nominal presence on the web. The web serves only as an information portal and place marker for GPII

Each time a part is ordered the customer service representative (CSR) finds the part, locates the part in the warehouse, and process the sale by writing a manual sales slip. Payments are also processed manually. If the part is out of stock, the CSR lets the purchasing clerk who orders the par and updates the card accordingly.

This Website is designed to provide information on the parts that we provide along with information about what’s new in the automotive industry via newspaper articles and blogs. Careers at our company can be found under the career tab. We hope that our site gives you the information you desire. You can contact us with any further questions by going to the Contact Info tab.

FAQ

Q: You don’t have my part. Now what?

A: If we don’t have it...we’ll find it. If we don’t have your foreign or domestic part in our inventory, we’ll locate if for you.

Q: What kind of parts do you stock in your facility?

A: We stock a huge variety of parts in our inventory. We specialize in cars that are 25 years or older and carry basic and unique parts for those cars.

Q: How do I begin a new search from the results screen?

A: You can simply push on the products tab to start a new search or click on the Global Parts International Inc. Logo at the top of your screen.

Q: My model is not listed in the Search options. Where can I find it?

A: You can try searching in broader terms such as “Ford” or “Dodge”. This will bring up anything we have for that model. If this still does not give you what you’re looking for please call our help number and we may be able to place an order for you.

Q: How do I return a part that I have ordered?

A: You can contact one of our Customer Service Representatives and they will get you through that process. Go to the contact us page for this information.
